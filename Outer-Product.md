# Outer Product

In linear algebra, the outer product of two coordinate vectors is the matrix whose entries are all products <br>
of an element in the first vector with an element in the second vector. If the two coordinate vectors have <br>
dimensions $`n`$ and $`m`$, then their outer product is an $`n \times m`$ matrix. <sup>[2]</sup>
